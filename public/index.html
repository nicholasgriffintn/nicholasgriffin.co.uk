About Me

Hello, I am Nicholas Griffin, and as you can see from my subheading, I am a web developer, blogger, and general technology enthusiast.

I have been blogging about technology since 2011 on the site I founded, called TechNutty. Since that time, I have been running pretty much everything to make sure TechNutty not only looks great and performs well but also provides the best service it can.

Much of the site has been designed by myself (aside from elements that are a bit more advanced), I also manage the server for TechNutty by myself and make sure everything is updated and working properly on a daily basis.

And if that wasn't enough, I also write my own posts on it.

But I'm not just about technology, as I also have a huge interest in business and politics. During my time at The University of Derby, I studied BA Business Management, on a course that was accredited by the CMI. This has provided me with a huge set of skills, that I use daily to manage the business side of TechNutty.
